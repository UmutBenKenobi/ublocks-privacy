UBlocks Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us

Last updated: February 15, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how UBlocks ("we", "our", "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile puzzle game available on iOS (App Store) and Android (Google Play).

By using UBlocks, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

📊 Data We Collect

Data Purpose Storage
Game progress (score, grid state, settings) Save & resume your game Local Only
User preferences (sound, haptics, theme) Personalize experience Local Only
Purchase status (undo, no-ads) Unlock paid features Local Only
Advertising ID (IDFA / GAID) Serve relevant ads via AdMob Third Party
Device info (OS, model, language) Ad targeting & crash analytics Third Party
Important: Game data (scores, settings, progress) is stored only on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not have servers that collect or store your personal gameplay data.

📰 Advertising (Google AdMob)

UBlocks displays ads through Google AdMob to support free gameplay. AdMob may collect the following:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
  • Device information (model, OS version, screen size)
  • IP address (for approximate location-based ads)
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings > Privacy > Tracking > disable for UBlocks
  • Android: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

You can also remove all ads permanently by purchasing the "No Ads" option within the App ($2.99, one-time).

For more information, see Google's Privacy Policy.

🔐 Your Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and other applicable laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about what data we process
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Opt out of personalized advertising

How to delete your data: Since all game data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all UBlocks data by uninstalling the app or clearing app data in your device settings. For AdMob data, contact Google directly or reset your advertising ID.

UBlocks is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will take steps to remove it.

However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.
